# Getting help

Thanks for trying Alpha Stick. Here is the fastest way to get unstuck.

## Where to ask

| You want to... | Go to |
|---|---|
| Ask a question, share a build, float an idea | [Discussions](https://github.com/owenpkent/alpha-stick/discussions) |
| Report a bug (firmware, tools, docs) | [Bug report](https://github.com/owenpkent/alpha-stick/issues/new?template=bug_report.yml) |
| Request a feature | [Feature request](https://github.com/owenpkent/alpha-stick/issues/new?template=feature_request.yml) |
| Share a print or bench result | [Build & bench report](https://github.com/owenpkent/alpha-stick/issues/new?template=build_report.yml) |
| Give accessibility feedback | [Accessibility feedback](https://github.com/owenpkent/alpha-stick/issues/new?template=accessibility_feedback.yml) |
| Report a security or safety issue | Privately — see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) |

## Before you post

- Skim the [Executive Summary](docs/EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY.md) and the relevant guide:
  [HARDWARE](docs/HARDWARE.md), [FIRMWARE](docs/FIRMWARE.md),
  [PRINTING](docs/PRINTING.md), [ASSEMBLY](docs/ASSEMBLY.md), or
  [CONFIGURATION](docs/CONFIGURATION.md).
- Search existing [issues](https://github.com/owenpkent/alpha-stick/issues) and
  [discussions](https://github.com/owenpkent/alpha-stick/discussions) first.
- Include specifics: for firmware, your ESP-IDF version and board; for prints,
  your printer, material, and slicer settings; for bench results, your
  measurement method and numbers.

This is a volunteer project in Phase 0 — please be patient and specific.
Accessibility feedback from people with disabilities is the most valuable input
we get; it outranks every estimate in the repo.
